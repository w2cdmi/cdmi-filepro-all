What a Permission Does a Team Member Use to Access a Team Space If the Member Is Assigned Multiple Permissions for the Team Space?

<<Back to Help Center

In the following scenarios, permissions are assigned to a team member.

  • One permission is assigned to the team member when it is added.
  • Second permission is assigned to the team member when the team of the member is entered to add members.
  • Third permission is assigned to the team member when you click Add All Users.

The team member uses the highest permission of the three permissions to access the team space.
