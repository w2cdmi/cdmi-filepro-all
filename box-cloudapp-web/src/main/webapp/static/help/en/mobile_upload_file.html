How Can I Upload a File from My Mobile Phone to StorBox?

<<Back to Help Center

StorBox for Android

Click the add button on the upper right of the screen and select the file format you want to add. You can create a folder and upload documents, photos, and all data in your mobile phone. You can also use the camera on your mobile phone to take pictures and upload them to StorBox.

StorBox for iOS

Click the add button on the upper right of the screen and select the file format you want to add. You can choose photos, videos, or using the camera on your mobile phone to take pictures/videos and upload them to StorBox.
