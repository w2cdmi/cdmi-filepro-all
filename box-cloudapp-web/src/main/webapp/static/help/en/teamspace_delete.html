How Can Delete the Team Space Created by Me?

<<Back to Help Center

  1. Log in to StorBox website.
  2. In the left navigation tree, click Team Space.
  3. Right-click the team space that you want to delete and choose Delete Team Space from the shortcut menu that is displayed.

    Or you can click the team space that you want to delete and click Delete Team Space on the menu bar in the upper area.

  4. In the Delete Team Space window, enter yes.

    After a team space is deleted, all data in the team space is deleted. Exercise caution when deleting a team space.

  5. Click OK.
