How Can I Remove a Recipient?

<<Back to Help Center

If you are the sharer, you can remove recipients. The recipient that is removed cannot access the shared file or folder and other recipients are not affected.

Perform the following steps to remove a recipient:

  1. Log in to StorBox website.
  2. Hover the cursor over the file or folder and click on the right.
    Note

    Alternatively, you can right-click the blank area to the right of the file or folder and choose Share or click Share above the file list to go to the Share window.

  3. Click to the right of the recipient.
