How Can I Change the Permissions of Team Space Members?

<<Back to Help Center

  1. Log in to StorBox website.
  2. In the left navigation tree, click Team Space.
  3. Right-click the team space and choose Manage Member from the shortcut menu that is displayed.

    Or you can click the team space and click Manage Member on the menu bar in the upper area.

  4. Click the drop-down arrow on the right of a team member to change its permission based on site requirements.
