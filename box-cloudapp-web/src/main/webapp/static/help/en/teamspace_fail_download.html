Why Cannot I Download Documents from the Team Space?

<<Back to Help Center

You have no permission to download documents from the team space. You can contact the owner of the team space to apply for the download permission. If you still cannot download documents after obtaining the permission, the enterprise administrator may configure a secure access control policy on the UAM for the team space. The control policy prevents you from downloading documents from the team space.
