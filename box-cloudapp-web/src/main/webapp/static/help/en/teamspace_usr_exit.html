How Can I Exit a Team Space?

<<Back to Help Center

  1. Log in to StorBox website.
  2. In the left navigation tree, click Team Space.
  3. Right-click the team space that you want to exit and choose Exit from the shortcut menu that is displayed.
  4. Click OK.
