How Can I Authorize a Folder in a Team Space and Add Members Who Can Upload Documents to and Edit Documents in the Team Space?

<<Back to Help Center

  1. Log in to StorBox website.
  2. In the left navigation tree, click Team Space.
  3. Double-click the icon of or click the name of a team space to go to the team space.
  4. Right-click the folder that you want to authorize and choose Authorize Folder from the shortcut menu that is displayed.
  5. In the Authorize Folder window that is displayed, click Click here to add more members.
  6. Select members that you want to add and set permissions for them.

    You can repeat steps 5 to 6 to add members based on site requirements.

  7. Click Close.
