How Can I Manage Team Space Members?

<<Back to Help Center

  1. Log in to StorBox website.
  2. In the left navigation tree, click Team Space.
  3. Right-click the team space whose members you want to manage and choose Manage Member from the shortcut menu that is displayed.

    Or you can click the team space whose members you want to manage and click Manage Member on the menu bar in the upper area.

  4. In the Manage Member window that is displayed, you can perform the following operations:
    • Viewing members and their permissions.
    • Adding new members: Enter the user names, names, and emails or team names, press Enter, and set permissions for users.
    • Deleting members: Click of the member that you want to delete.
    • Modifying permissions of members: Click the drop-down arrow of the member whose permission you want to modify and set new permission for the member.
