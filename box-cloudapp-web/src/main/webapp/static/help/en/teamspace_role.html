How Many Permissions Are There for a Team Space?

<<Back to Help Center

On the UAM, the enterprise administrator can set permissions of users for accessing team spaces. (Owners and administrators of team spaces have default access permissions, and the enterprise administrator cannot modify their permissions.)

The permissions for team spaces are as follows:

  • Owner: creator of a team space. Each team space belongs to one owner only. This role can dismiss the team space, change the owner of the team space, manage the team space, share links of documents, and browse, preview, upload, download, and edit documents.
  • Manager: This role can manage team space members, share links of documents, and browse, preview, upload, download, and edit documents.
  • Editor: This role can share links of documents and browse, preview, upload, download, and edit documents.
  • Uploadable and viewable: This permission allows users to browse, preview, upload, and download documents.
  • Viewer: This role can browse, preview, and download documents.
  • Previewer: This role can preview and browse documents.
  • Uploader: This role can browse and upload documents.
