Can I Play Videos on My StorBox for Android/iOS?

<<Back to Help Center

StorBox for Android

StorBox for Android uses the video player on your Android device to play videos. Details of the video player depend on your Android device, operating system version, and video player type.

If some video formats are not supported, download a new video player.

StorBox for iOS

After videos are downloaded from cloud to mobile phones, StorBox for iOS can play the MPEG4 files with the H.264 video code and the advanced audio coding (ACC) audio code.
